Energy Market Hedge

15-yr PPA fixed at €0.068/kWh — locked before construction. EU wholesale spot prices ranged from around €0.03/kWh in normal periods to monthly averages of €0.40–0.50/kWh during the 2022 crisis, with daily spikes above €0.70/kWh. We are immune. Three stress scenarios modelled (€0.15 / €0.25 / €0.35/kWh spot) — project viable in all.
