> - Modular design — scalable from day one
> - Tier III redundancy (N+1 on all critical systems)
> - Liquid cooling core · air-side economisation

**Script — Person B:**
> "Thank you. The architecture we've designed for Birdy Capital is modular — meaning it scales with your business without a rebuild. At the core: Tier III redundancy across power, cooling, and connectivity. No single point of failure. For cooling — and this is where we make a significant efficiency gain — we use a liquid cooling core for high-density server racks, combined with air-side economisation for the perimeter. In practical terms, that gives us a Power Usage Effectiveness of 1.2, compared to the industry average of 1.6. That's 25% less energy wasted — which directly reduces your operating costs and your carbon footprint. Your portal has the full site-specific schematics."
