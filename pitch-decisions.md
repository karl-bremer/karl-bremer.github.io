# English Oral Exam — Pitch Decisions

## Case
Case 14: Sustainable Data Centre — €50M, 4 years

## Format
Client pitch — teacher plays the investor/client commissioning the data centre.
Visual aid: website (public + private client portal) + PowerPoint deck.
Duration: ~25 minutes.

## Roles
- **Person A — Account Director:** client relationship, business case, financials, open and close
- **Person B — Technical Director:** technical architecture, sustainability, risk

## Slide Structure

| # | Title | Speaker | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Title slide | — | — |
| 2 | Who we are | A | 2 min |
| 3 | Your brief, back to you | A | 3 min |
| 4 | Technical Architecture | B | 5 min |
| 5 | Energy Mix | B | — |
| 6 | Sustainability | B | 4 min |
| 7 | Risk Register | B | 3 min |
| 8 | Financial Overview | A | 4 min |
| 9 | Roadmap | A | 2 min |
| 10 | Next Steps | A | 2 min |

**Handover A → B** after slide 3: "Let me bring in our Technical Director..."
**Handover B → A** after slide 7: "I'll pass back to [A] for the business case..."

## Website Structure

### Public (visible to anyone)
- Homepage — firm name, tagline, hero image
- About Us — team, bios, roles
- Portfolio — 2–3 past projects
- Our Technology — general approach (renewable, cooling, servers, circular lifecycle)
- Sustainability Commitment — philosophy, EU framework, certifications

### Private Client Portal (password-protected)
- Your Brief — her requirements reflected back with our commitments
- Your Technical Design — site-specific architecture, energy mix, cooling choice
- Your Sustainability Report — carbon trajectory, EU compliance checklist
- Financial Proposal — full €50M breakdown, ROI, contingency
- Risk Register — 4 risks with likelihood/impact/mitigation
- Your 4-Year Roadmap — phased milestones, decision points
- Next Steps — 3 steps to kick off, contact details

## Budget Breakdown (Financial Slide)

| Category | Budget | % |
|---|---|---|
| Civil works & infrastructure | €18M | 36% |
| Renewable energy systems | €12M | 24% |
| IT hardware & servers | €10M | 20% |
| Cooling systems | €6M | 12% |
| Project management & compliance | €4M | 8% |

## Key Design Rules
- One idea per slide, max 3 bullets, max 6 words each
- Key numbers large and prominent
- Website and deck share same colors, fonts, logo
- Say "see your portal for details" when slides are intentionally light
